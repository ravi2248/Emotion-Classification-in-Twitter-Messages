Error Analysis Insights

A qualitative review of the model's errors revealed that most mistakes occur between emotions with significant vocabulary overlap. For example, the model often misclassifies text expressing personal happiness ('joy') as 'love' when words like "blessed," "generous," or "passionate" are used. This highlights the complexity of language and suggests that even advanced models can be challenged by nuanced expressions.
